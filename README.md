Monetary Economics & QPM Simulator: Final Exam Engine 🏦📊An advanced, fully interactive Monetary Economics Dashboard and Examination Engine designed for undergraduate and MBA programs. This tool shifts the pedagogical paradigm from passive theoretical learning to active macroeconomic simulation.Developed for the School of Digital Technologies & Economics (SDTE) at AlmaU (Almaty Management University).🌟 Core FeaturesThis platform is built on a dynamic architecture that personalizes the exam experience based on the student's assigned Central Bank (e.g., PBoC, BoJ, BCRA, SNB, Riksbank).1. Dynamic Theory & Framework EngineAutomatically generates theory questions and framework parameters based on the specific Central Bank assigned to the student.Tests knowledge on operational instruments, intermediate targets, and monetary aggregates specific to real-world nations.2. Interactive Balance Sheet (T-Accounts)A live, double-entry accounting simulator.Students perform Open Market Operations (OMO), FX interventions, and Quantitative Easing (QE), while the system algorithmically checks for balance sheet consolidation in real-time.3. The Impossible Trilemma GeometryAn interactive SVG-based triangle representing the Mundell-Fleming Trilemma.Students must map their assigned Central Bank's policy stance (Pure Float, Fixed Peg, or Capital Controls) directly onto the macroeconomic model.4. QPM (Quarterly Projection Model) SimulatorA simplified, browser-based New Keynesian macroeconomic model.Features an active Taylor Rule response curve, IS Curve (Output Gap with Kalman filtering logic), and Phillips Curve (Inflation expectations).Students inject exogenous shocks (Demand, Supply, Oil Prices) and analyze the central bank's simulated policy rate response.5. Cryptographic Academic Integrity (The Passport Token)Zero server required: To prevent cheating and unauthorized sharing, the system compiles the student's entire session matrix, answers, and simulated data into a highly secure, encoded Base64 token.Professor Dashboard: Instructors use a secure master key to unlock a hidden decryption panel, paste the student's token, and instantly generate an automated, detailed grading report.🛠 Tech StackHTML5 / CSS3Tailwind CSS: For a premium, dark-mode adaptive, and highly responsive UI.Chart.js: For high-performance, real-time rendering of macroeconomic curves (Inflation, Output Gap, Policy Rate).Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+): Complete client-side logic, simulation engine, and cryptographic token generation without the need for a backend database.🎓 Methodology & PedagogyThis engine embraces the "Learning by Doing" philosophy. Rather than memorizing the effects of an interest rate hike, students are forced to mathematically execute it, watch the output gap shrink on a live chart, and justify their actions in a policy brief. It bridges the gap between abstract monetary theory and applied central banking.👨‍🏫 AuthorDmitry OgayProfessor of Practice, Managerial & Monetary EconomicsAlmaty Management University (AlmaU)
